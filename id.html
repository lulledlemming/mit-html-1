Marksmen

Marksmen excel at dealing reliable sustained damage at range (usually through basic attacks) while constantly skirting the edge of danger.

Mages

Mages are offensive casters that seek to cripple and burn down the opposition through their potent spells.

Fighters

Fighters are durable and damage-focused melee champions that look to be in the thick of combat.-->

Assassins

Assassins are fragile but agile damage-focused melee champions that look to swiftly take down their targets.
